SUSE Rancher Prime requirements

1. Minimum hardware requirements

Nodes for HA setup

At least 3 nodes.

RAM

A minimum of 32 GB of RAM.

CPU

At least 8 CPU cores.

Disk space

At least 200 GB of storage, preferably SSD.
